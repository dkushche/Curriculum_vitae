\section{DevOps}

\subsection{Description}

I am passionate about the DevOps approach and try to apply it in most of my projects.
It helps keep systems manageable, maintainable, and easy to iterate on. I use DevOps
tools in both commercial and non-commercial projects, and when I cannot find a tool that
fits my needs, I am able to create my own using Python or Golang.

Level of proficiency: Intermediate Mid
