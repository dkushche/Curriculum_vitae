\subsection{Description}

I have extensive commercial experience as an Embedded Engineer.
I worked on multiple projects involving microcontrollers, SoCs,
and IoT devices. My responsibilities included firmware development,
implementing communication protocols, performing FOTA/OTA updates,
and ensuring reliable device onboarding and connectivity.
