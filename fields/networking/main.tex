\section{Networking}

\subsection{Description}

I have broad experience with networking across all layers of the OSI model. I have worked with numerous protocols
and software used in WiFi, wired, and virtual networks. I am passionate about distributed networks and have hands-on
experience with peer-to-peer and mesh technologies. My work includes network configuration, administration, simulation,
security, and overlay networks.

\subsection{Level of proficiency}

Level of proficiency: Intermediate High
